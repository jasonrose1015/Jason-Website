Upgrades Incoming for Game Machine XI

Like any other PC build, this one has been a process. I started this build back in 2016 and have slowly added pieces and upgraded the CPU and GPU when possible. What started out as an i7-6700K / GTX 970 combo, has evolved into an i7-8700K / GTX 1070 combo. Over this time, I have also added 2 x 256GB Samasung 850 EVO SSDs. Until now, I have not made the upgrade to M.2 NVMe SSDs. So this year, that is my next upgrade. I will be adding the Samsung 970 EVO 500GB M.2 drive. The drive is priced at $135 on most websites. I am also going to add another 16GB of RAM. I will be doing a video over the upgrade.

Current Build - Game Machine XI - January 2019

When it comes to tech, there's nothing like building your own PC. While it may be easier to buy a pre-built one, the satisfaction isn't the same. You also get a lot more for your money while learning a lot about the components. I always enjoy upgrading my PC during the winter months, specifically during the holidays. During this time, prices tend to be a little cheaper on most components, making it the perfect time to upgrade or build.
