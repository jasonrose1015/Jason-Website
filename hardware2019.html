Intel Core i7-6700K and SLI MSI GTX 970 Build (2015)

This build was completed for my brother, Timothy. At the time, he was in college. He wanted a system that would allow him to complete projects and play some high-end games. We added 2 x GTX 970s for SLI, 16GB of RAM and an SSD for awesome load times.

Intel Core i7-6700K and EVGA GTX 970 Build (2016)

This was a personal build using the Thermaltake Core P5 case. It is a unique case, with an open air cooling style. I added the Corsair H100i AiO to help cool the CPU.

Intel Core i7-8700K and EVGA GTX 1070 FTW2 iCX and Corsair 570X Build (2017)

After the Thermaltake Core P5 Build, I decided to upgrade my case when Corsair announced the 570X. This case features four tempered glass panels and is capable of holding 360mm AiO radiators at the top and in the front.
