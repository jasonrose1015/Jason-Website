Meltdown and Spectre

Meltdown and Spectre take advantage of critical vulnerabilities in processors. These weaknesses have been found in both AMD and Intel processors. While the major concern will be the newer CPUs, older CPUs are also vulnerable. AMD has said that their processors will be patched via a software update. However, Intel will need a software and firmware update. It is said that in some cases, the patches will reduce performance by thirty percent. Intel Announces Several New Coffee Lake CPUs

Over the next two months, Intel will release several new CPUs and chipsets for budget motherboards. Intel Coffee Lake platform will soon receive more SKUs, including Core i5-8600 (nonK), Core i5-8500, Core i3-8300 and at least five Pentiums and Celerons. The launch of the new budget CPUs will be accompanied by cheaper motherboards, such as B360 or H370. For Pentium and Celeron processors, you may want to wait for H310. This chipset will be the mainstream platform for them. Estimated cost for the new CPUs are looking to be $220-$200 for new i5 CPUs. The list of new CPUs and pricing are in the article locatedhere.
